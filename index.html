Sacred Heritage

The Art of Devotion

Preserving classical purity through generations.

Shivaruthya Natyalaya was founded on the auspicious occasion of Vijayadashami, a day celebrated as a symbol of knowledge, art, and new beginnings. Established in 2025, our academy is dedicated to nurturing young talents and preserving the timeless tradition of Bharatanatyam.

Founded with the core vision of maintaining technical precision, emotional depth (Bhava), and absolute devotion, our academy passes on the ancient wisdom of rhythm (Tala) and expression (Abhinaya) in its most authentic form.

Curriculum & Programs

Nurturing Divine Talent

Our courses are structured into rigorous levels, ensuring students of all age groups and skill levels build a strong foundation in theory, rhythm, and classical expression.

Kids & Beginners

Bala Nritya

Introduction to basic steps (Adavus), head, neck, and eye movements (Shiro, Griva, and Drishti bhedas), and single-hand mudras (Asamyuta Hastas).

2 Hours / Week Age 5+
Intermediate

Margam Pravesha

Developing complexity with combined footwork patterns, double-hand mudras (Samyuta Hastas), and learning the first items of the classical Margam: Alarippu, Jatiswaram, and Shabdam.

3 Hours / Week Age 8+
Advanced

Arangetram Sadhana

Mastering complex expressional items (Varnam, Padam, Ashtapadi, Tillana). Dedicated preparation for solo debut performances (Arangetram) and stage presentation.

5 Hours / Week Inv. Only
The Acharya

Guru Tharuthrithiya Sivaraj

MFA (Bharatanatyam) | Founder & Artistic Director of Nithyanatyalaya

Guru Tharuthrithiya Sivaraj, decorated with prestigious titles like Natyasironmani, Kala busitha, and Divya desa nirthya kala bushan, is a passionate Bharatanatyam exponent and Nattuvangam expert. Holding a Master of Fine Arts (MFA) in Bharatanatyam from Annamalai University under the mentorship of distinguished classical masters, Guru Tharuthrithiya has spent over a decade teaching, choreographing, and nurturing this divine art form.

"Dance is not just motion—it is the language of devotion, connecting the mortal seeker to the ultimate cosmos through the purity of mudras, expressions, and rhythm."
  • Mastered Nattuvangam under traditional classical exponents
  • Performed at the sacred Chidambaram Natyanjali Festival
  • Successfully staged 30+ Natyanjali programs across Tamil Nadu
  • Honored as "Promising Young Dancer" by Chennai Dance Circle
  • Performer in 4 Bharatanatyam World Record events

Academy Leadership

  • Shivaruthyanatyalaya: Founder and Artistic Director since Jan 2020. Manages academies at Chennai Harbor and Nolambur SNP Gardens, training students in Bharatanatyam theory, postures, expressions, and stage performance.
  • Nithyanatyalaya: Instructor since 2014 (Redhills). Successfully trained and coordinated traditional Salangai Pooja debuts for multiple student batches (Jan 2023 & Nov 2025).
  • School Coaching: Taught and choreographed school programs at Badal Chand Jain Vidyalaya Mat. School and Kaniyalal Agarwal Bal Niketan.

Education & Lineage

  • MFA in Bharatanatyam: Master of Fine Arts degree from Annamalai University (2021-2023) guided by leading academic and classical dance veterans.
  • Nattuvangam Specialization: Completed 3 levels of advanced Nattuvangam training under traditional masters.
  • Graded Certification: Successfully completed seven grades in Bharatanatyam from the Bridge Academy.
  • English Literature (BA): Holds a Bachelor's degree from Patrician College of Arts & Science, Madras University.

Festivals & Media

  • Natyanjali Festivals: Performed in Chidambaram Natyanjali (2023) and Thiruvathirai festivals, completing 30+ Natyanjali programs across Tamil Nadu temples.
  • Television Appearances: Solo dancer featured on Zee Tamil’s Chennaiil Thiruvaiyaru and Podhigai Channel’s Pongal celebrations.
  • Choreographic Honors: Led school teams to 1st place in city-level classical dance competitions; Awarded Best Dancer at St. Anne's.
Vijayadashami Inauguration

The Sacred Beginning of Shivaruthya Natyalaya

On the auspicious occasion of Vijayadashami, a day that symbolizes wisdom, art, and new beginnings, Shivaruthya Natyalaya embarked on its journey in 2025. With the blessings of our gurus and the rich heritage of Bharatanatyam, this inauguration marks the first step toward nurturing young talents, inspiring artistic excellence, and preserving the timeless traditions of Indian classical dance for generations to come.
